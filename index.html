Our Story Begins with a Single Bean

Founded in 2018, Velvet Bean started as a small roastery in a converted warehouse. Today, we source beans directly from ethical farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Sumatra.

Every batch is roasted in small quantities to ensure peak freshness. We believe coffee is more than a drink — it's a ritual, a connection, a moment of peace in a busy world.
